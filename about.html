OUR STORY

Bringing the authentic taste of a home-cooked "Dabba" to the heart of Namma Bengaluru.

The Heart of Namma Bengaluru

Savi Dabba was born from a simple mission: to provide wholesome, preservative-free meals that remind you of home. We believe that food should be an experience, not just a convenience. From the early morning steam of our **Idlys** to the late-night aroma of our **Donne Biryanis**, every dish is crafted with care.

Whether you are looking for a **South Indian Thali** for lunch or a quick **Lime Soda** to beat the heat, we bring you the flavors you love, delivered fresh to your doorstep.

Fresh Ingredients

We use only the freshest fruit extracts and produce for our juices and meals.

Authentic Recipes

Our Biryanis are dum-cooked to perfection using traditional methods.
